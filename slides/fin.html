What didn't we do?

                

                    
Our architecture isn't very object-oriented(harder to reuse code).

                    
Because it's not very object oriented, we didn't document anything.

                    
We should use "endpoints" that get data out of our database, instead of having view-specific code.

                    
No server-side validation! Holy crap!

                    
Lots of error handling we didn't do.

                

                
Most of these problems are on purpose - partly to give you something to work on, and partly because it was easier to understand a flow of functions than a fully object oriented architecture.
